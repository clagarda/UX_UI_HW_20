Play Travel Case Study

Me and my partner worked diligently to create a Travel Application where a person could to book vacations, all-inclusive. As well as, find travel destination based on likes and wants!

Research

During our research phase, we conducted user testing to create a proto-persona of who exactly we are targeting. We also created a survey from which we gathered key insights on our site.

  • Our site needed to be easy to navigate
  • Competetors show less "all-inclusive" resorts
  • Activity Sorting would be a great feature
  • Family-Oriented

Problem Statement

Our travel application was created and designed for the purpose of giving planners an easier and more enjoyable experience when traveling post-pandemic. We have observed that our current application doesn't meet the needs of adventurers who need to plan out every aspect of their trip in one spot including expenses, activities, and hotels. How might we offer resources to travelers with safety concerns and give resources for the things they plan?

Value Proposition

Our organization is developing a travel app to help target customers who are in need of intense planning including but not limited to dates available to travel, activities available to plan, and family-diverse fun to solve vacation planners frustrations of not knowing where and what to do when going somewhere.
